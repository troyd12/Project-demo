Follow these 4 simple steps to get your site live

1

Open Your AI Project Folder

3

Drag & Drop the Folder

Drag the entire td-movement-site folder from your file explorer and drop it onto the Netlify Drop zone in your browser.

⬇

✅ Deploying your site... Done!

⚠️ Important:

Drag the folder itself (td-movement-site), NOT the individual files inside it. Netlify needs the whole folder.

4

Connect Your Custom Domain

After the deploy finishes, Netlify gives you a random URL (like dapper-tulumba-14656d.netlify.app). To point tdmovement.com to this new deploy:

1. Click "Site settings" on your new site

2. Go to "Domain management"

3. Click "Add custom domain"

4. Type: tdmovement.com

5. Netlify will ask to move it from your old project — click "Yes, move domain"

🎉 Your TD Movement site is now live at tdmovement.com!

💡 Alternative:

Instead of a new deploy, you can update your existing tdmovement.com project directly. Go to app.netlify.com → click the tdmovement.com project → Deploys tab → drag your folder into the deploy zone at the bottom of the page. This keeps your domain connected automatically.
